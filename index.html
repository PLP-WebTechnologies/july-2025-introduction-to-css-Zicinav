About Sage

Sage is a playful Great Dane with a heart as big as his paws. Even though he is a giant, he is still a puppy at heart, always eager to run, play, and make new friends.

He loves cuddles, long walks, and chasing after squeaky toys. His gentle nature makes him a beloved companion, and his goofy antics bring joy to everyone around him.

Why Sage is Special

  • Size: Towering but gentle
  • Personality: Playful, affectionate, and loyal
  • Hobbies: Chasing balls, napping in sunny spots
  • Skills: Puppy eyes that melt hearts
